Data Handler

A data handler, also known as a data controller or data processor depending on the context, is an entity or organization responsible for collecting, processing, and storing personal data. They must follow rules to keep your information safe and use it properly. For example, they can't share it with others without your permission, and they must make sure it's kept secure.

Or are you a?

Data Subject

A data subject is just a fancy term for you or anyone else whose personal information is being collected, processed, or stored. So, if an organization has your name and address in their records, you're the data subject. You have rights over your personal information, like being able to ask what information they have about you or to request they delete it if you don't want them to have it anymore.
